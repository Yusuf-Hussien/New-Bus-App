## üÜò Troubleshooting

### If pages aren't indexed after 2 weeks:
1. Check robots.txt isn't blocking pages
2. Verify sitemap.xml is accessible
3. Check for crawl errors in Search Console
4. Ensure pages are accessible (no login required for public pages)
5. Add more backlinks

### Common Issues:
- **403/404 errors**: Fix server configuration
- **robots.txt blocking**: Check your robots.txt file
- **No sitemap**: Ensure sitemap.xml is accessible at `/sitemap.xml`
